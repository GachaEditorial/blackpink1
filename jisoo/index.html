about jisoo

Kim Ji-soo, better known as Jisoo, is a South Korean singer. Jisoo became a YG Entertainment trainee in August 2011, when she was 16 years old, she trained for five years for her singing career. In August 2016, she became a member of BLACKPINK, as a lead vocalist and visualist in the group.
