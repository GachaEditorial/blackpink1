about rosé

Roseanne Park, better known as Rosé, is an Australian singer and dancer, currently based in South Korea. As a child, Rosé already had a passion for singing and was a part of her church's choir. At the YG Entertainment auditions in Sydney, Australia in 2012, she ranked first in the auditions, and she became a part of YG Entertainment on the same day.
