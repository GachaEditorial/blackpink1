about lisa

Lalisa Manoban, also known as Lisa, is a Thai singer, rapper, dancer and model. She was born on March 27, 1997 in Bangkok, Thailand. Between 29 September and 29 October 2010, the first YG Entertainment audition was held in Thailand, where she reached 1st place and was accepted as the only candidate in YG Entertainment. She also became the first non-Korean member of YG Entertainment. She is fluent in Thai, Korean, Japanese and English.
